Top 10 Best Books

  1. The Great Gatsby

    The Great Gatsby is a novel by F. Scott Fitzgerald that follows the life of Jay Gatsby, a wealthy man who throws extravagant parties in the hopes of winning back his former love, Daisy Buchanan.

  2. To Kill a Mockingbird

    To Kill a Mockingbird is a novel by Harper Lee that tells the story of Scout Finch, a young girl growing up in a small town in Alabama during the 1930s, and her father, Atticus, a lawyer who defends a black man accused of rape.

  3. Pride and Prejudice

    Pride and Prejudice is a novel by Jane Austen that tells the story of Elizabeth Bennet, a young woman who must navigate the social conventions of Georgian England in order to find love and happiness.

  4. 1984

    1984 is a dystopian novel by George Orwell that depicts a totalitarian society where the government controls every aspect of citizens' lives and even their thoughts.

  5. The Catcher in the Rye

    The Catcher in the Rye is a novel by J.D. Salinger that follows the life of Holden Caulfield, a teenage boy who is struggling to come to terms with his own identity and the world around him.
